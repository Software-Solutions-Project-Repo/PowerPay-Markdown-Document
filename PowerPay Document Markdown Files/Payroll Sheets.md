# Payroll Sheets 

## What are payroll sheets? 

In the industry, payroll sheets are compiled on excel spreadsheets or using external financial software. This part of the application aims to simplify that process with dynamic payroll sheets. In this dynamic payroll sheets, It will show employee number,  employee name, wages, period across which they are paid, overtime  and the total amount. 

#

## How to view the payroll sheets?

First, you must select the Pay Frequency and Pay Period. 

![][image4]

**Pay Period**   
This is the period that is configured in the configuration settings. This will depend on your Company’s calendar.
   
**Pay Frequency**  
Pay frequency is based on how regular your employees are paid. The frequencies available are weekly, biweekly and monthly. These frequencies are based on the Pay Period.   
Weekly is where employees receive a pay cheque at the end of the week. This can be done for 52 weeks or less depending on your Company’s policy. Weekly may take into account overtime.  
Biweekly is where employees are paid every two weeks in a 26 weeks pay period.   
Monthly is where employees are paid on a monthly basis where they will receive 12 payments each year. 

After selecting the pay frequency and pay period, you can proceed by choosing the **Load Paysheets** option. This will display a list of employees who worked during that specific period, according to the selected frequency.

![][image5]

### Payroll Summary

After a paysheet has been loaded, a payroll summary will be displayed. The payroll summary shows the total number employees, total normal hours worked, total overtime worked and total net pay. 

### Employee Paysheet

Under payroll summary, the employee paysheets will be displayed as a table. The employee paysheet lists the following information:
- Employee Number (Emp No)
- Name
- Template (this is the template that is used for that employee)
- Pay Frequency (Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly)
- Pay Period
- Normal Time Worked (NT)
- Over Time Worked (OT)
- Double Time Worked (DT)
- Triple Time Worked (TT)
- Amount (Total Amount of money paid)


## How to edit payroll sheet?

If you wish to edit the current template, you can select the edit button and you will be able to edit the values in the sheet
