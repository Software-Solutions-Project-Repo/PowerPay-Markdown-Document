# Payroll Cycles

## What are Payroll Cycles?

Payroll cycles are schedules  that determine how frequently employee wages are calculated and disbursed. These cycles define both the pay frequency (weekly, biweekly, or monthly) and the corresponding pay periods covered by each payroll run.

## How to Create a Payroll Cycle 

You can create a payroll cycle by selecting + New Cycle.

{insert image here}

When selected, you must fill in details such as:  
- Description   
- Pay Frequency   
- Period  
- Payment Date   
- Payment Settings (Cash, Cheque and Bank)
 > Note: If you select Cheque, you will be prompted to put a Starting Cheque Number
